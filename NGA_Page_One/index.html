The Age of Modern Art

Is there a peak age for creativity? When do artists produce their most popular works? Who are some of the youngest and oldest artists to have their work displayed in the NGA's collection of modern art?

Although the majority of artworks from the 20th century were made by artists in their 40s to 60s, the range of creative expression is much wider — both 13-year-olds and 93-year-olds have left an enduring impact with their work. By showcasing both the average age and the outliers, I hope to shift perceptions and expectations regarding age limits and artistic accomplishment.
